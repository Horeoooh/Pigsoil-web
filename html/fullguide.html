Basic Pig Manure Composting

Complete step-by-step guide for beginners

About This Method

Traditional aerobic composting using pig waste and plant matter. Perfect for beginners with minimal equipment needed. Creates low-cost organic fertilizer in 21 days.

Materials Needed

Fresh pig manure
Dry plant matter
Shovel or fork
Rice hulls/sawdust
Water source
Tarpaulin cover

Step-By-Step Process

1

Layer pig manure & plant matter

Day 1

Create alternating layers of fresh pig manure and dry plant materials. Start with a 6-inch layer of plant matter, then 4 inches of manure. Repeat 3-4 times.

Tip:
Use a 3:1 ratio of brown materials (leaves, rice hulls) to green materials (fresh manure)
